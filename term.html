Refund policy

Have a problem with your order? We’ve got your back with our 25-day return policy. If you’re not satisfied with your Project Beauty online purchase, or if it arrives damaged, simply follow the steps below to return the unused portion and we’ll gladly issue a refund or send you a replacement.

  • Step 2: Complete the Return Request Form and pack it with the product(s) along with a copy of your invoice.
  • Step 3: Ship it back to us using a carrier of your choice. We recommend using an insured and traceable delivery method. Project Beauty is not responsible for any returns lost in transit.

    B&R Products Inc.
    Attn: Returns

    Miami, FL. 33157

    4. We will refund the original form of payment to the original purchaser within 48 to 72 hours of receiving your return. We regret that we are unable to refund the original shipping charges.
    5. If you have questions about returns or exchanges, please contact us via email at info@myprojectbeauty.com.
